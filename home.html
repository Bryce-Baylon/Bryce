HISTORY OF
WORLD WAR 2

World War 2 (1939-1945) was a global conflict involving most of the worlds nations,
including major powers like the Allies (led by the USA, USSR, and the UK)
and the Axis (led by Nazi Germany, Fascist Italy, and Imperial Japan).
It was the deadliest conflict in history, resulting from
political and economic instability following World War 1,
the rise of totalitarian regimes, and territorial ambitions.
Key events include the Holocaust, the bombing of Pearl Harbor,
and the atomic bombings of Nagasaki and Hiroshima. The war ended
with the defeat of the Axis powers, reshaping the global order.
